> About Our Institute

Opened in 2022, Education Beginner's IT School provides high-quality IT training for beginners at a low price. We are committed to making IT education accessible to everyone. Our training is provided in Hindi to ensure students can learn in a comfortable and familiar language.

We offer a variety of courses, from fundamental programming to advanced DevOps and Cloud technologies, helping students become "zero to hero."
